### Brand Voice
Upload 3+ samples of your existing content → Jasper analyzes and learns your style → all future content uses your brand voice.

**For Ukrainian agencies:** Create separate brand voices for each client. When generating content for Client A, select their voice — output sounds like Client A, not generic AI content.

Conversational AI similar to Claude/ChatGPT but specialized for marketing. Better at marketing tasks (writing ads, emails, social posts) than general AI assistants, but less capable for research and analysis.
