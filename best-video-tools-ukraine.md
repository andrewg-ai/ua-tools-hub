## 1. CapCut — Best for Ukrainian Social Media

CapCut is the dominant short-form video editor in Ukraine. Owned by ByteDance (TikTok's parent company), it's optimized for creating TikTok, Instagram Reels, and YouTube Shorts content.

**Why Ukrainian creators use CapCut:**
- Auto-captions with Ukrainian language support
- Trending templates updated daily
- AI background removal
- Text-to-speech in Ukrainian
- CapCut web version works in browser
- Completely free with no watermark on most features

**Business use cases:**
- Product demo videos
- Behind-the-scenes content
- Testimonial videos
- Tutorial content
- Announcement videos

**Free plan:** Full editing features, no watermark on exports

---

## 2. DaVinci Resolve — Best Free Professional Editor

DaVinci Resolve is Hollywood-grade video editing software available completely free. Used in professional film and TV production, it's more capable than Adobe Premiere Pro's standard features — at no cost.

**Why it's growing in Ukraine:**
- Completely free with all professional features
- Color grading tools used in Netflix productions
- Fusion (built-in motion graphics/VFX)
- Fairlight (built-in audio mixing studio)
- Collaboration features for teams

**Limitation:** Requires a powerful computer — GPU with 8GB+ VRAM recommended for 4K editing. Steep learning curve compared to CapCut.

**Paid version (Studio, $295 one-time):** AI noise reduction, more collaboration features — most Ukrainian users are served by the free version.

---

## 3. Runway — AI Video Generation

Runway's Gen-3 model generates video from text prompts or extends existing video with AI. Ukrainian marketers use it for creating B-roll footage and visual content without a camera crew.

**What Ukrainian businesses create:**
- Product visualizations
- Abstract brand videos
- Background footage for presentations
- Social media visual content

**Pricing:** Standard $15/month (625 credits), Pro $35/month (2,250 credits)

---

## 4. Descript — Edit Video by Editing Text

Descript transcribes your video and lets you edit by editing the text transcript. Delete a word from the transcript — that segment is removed from video. Revolutionary for talking-head content.

**For Ukrainian businesses:**
- Remove filler words ("uh", "um") automatically
- Edit presentations and webinar recordings by transcript
- Overdub: re-record specific words with AI voice clone
- Screen recording with automatic highlight detection
- Podcast production with studio sound enhancement

**Ukrainian note:** Transcription works in Ukrainian but less accurately than English. Best for English-language content or Ukrainian content where perfect transcript accuracy isn't critical.

**Pricing:** Free (1 hour transcription/month), Creator $24/month

---

## 5. Loom — Best for Ukrainian Remote Teams

Loom records screen and camera simultaneously — perfect for async communication in remote Ukrainian teams. AI features summarize videos and generate chapters automatically.

**Ukrainian remote work use cases:**
- Replacing meetings with video updates
- Client onboarding and tutorials
- Bug reports with screen recording
- Sales outreach personalization
- Internal process documentation

**Free plan:** 25 videos, 5 minutes each
**Business:** $15/month, unlimited videos

---

## 6. Synthesia — AI Avatar Videos

Synthesia creates professional videos with AI avatars — no camera, no filming, no studio. Type your script, choose an avatar, generate video in minutes.

**Ukrainian business use cases:**
- Product explainer videos
- Corporate training content
- Multilingual content (same script in 120+ languages)
- Customer onboarding videos

**Ukrainian language:** Synthesia supports Ukrainian text-to-speech. AI avatars speak Ukrainian convincingly.

**Pricing:** Starter $29/month (10 videos)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What video editing software do Ukrainian YouTubers use?**
A: CapCut for short-form content, DaVinci Resolve for long-form YouTube videos. Adobe Premiere Pro is used by professional Ukrainian video producers but the monthly subscription cost pushes many toward DaVinci's free version.

**Q: Can I create Ukrainian-language video captions automatically?**
A: Yes — CapCut has the best Ukrainian auto-caption support. Simply upload your video with Ukrainian audio, enable auto-captions, and review/correct. Accuracy is around 85-90% for clear Ukrainian speech.

**Q: What's the best video tool for Ukrainian Instagram Reels?**
A: CapCut is purpose-built for this use case — trending templates, ratio presets, auto-captions, and direct export to Instagram format. Most popular Ukrainian Instagram creators use CapCut for Reels.

## Bottom Line

Start with **CapCut** (free) for social media and **DaVinci Resolve** (free) for professional editing — this free combination covers 90% of Ukrainian business video needs.

Add **Loom** for team communication and **Descript** for talking-head content editing as your video production matures.
