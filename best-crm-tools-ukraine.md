## 1. HubSpot CRM — Best Free CRM for Ukrainian Startups

HubSpot's free CRM is genuinely powerful — contact management, deal pipeline, email tracking, and meeting scheduling at no cost forever.

**Free forever includes:**
- Unlimited contacts and companies
- Deal pipeline management
- Email tracking and notifications
- Meeting scheduling link
- Live chat and chatbot
- Form builder
- Basic reporting

**Why Ukrainian startups choose HubSpot:**
- Best free tier in the CRM market — covers most startup needs
- Marketing, Sales, and Service tools in one platform
- Integrates with Gmail, Google Calendar, Outlook
- No per-user limits on free plan

**Limitations of free plan:** Basic automation only, HubSpot branding on forms/chat, limited reporting

**Paid plans:** Starter $20/month (2 users), Professional $500/month

**[→ Start HubSpot CRM Free](https://hubspot.com/crm)**

---

## 2. Bitrix24 — Best for Ukrainian SMBs

Bitrix24 is enormously popular among Ukrainian businesses — it has Ukrainian language support, local customer service, and a feature set that covers CRM, project management, HR, and communication in one platform.

**Why Ukrainian businesses choose Bitrix24:**
- Ukrainian language interface ✅
- Free plan includes unlimited users (unique among CRMs)
- CRM + Task management + HR + Communication in one
- Ukrainian telephony integration
- Self-hosted option available (data in Ukraine)

**Features:**
- Lead and deal management
- Email and phone call logging
- Sales automation
- Document management
- Internal communication (chat, video)
- HR management and time tracking

**Pricing:** Free (unlimited users, basic features), Basic $49/company/month

---

## 3. amoCRM — Ukrainian Market Favorite

amoCRM (now kommo.com) has a large Ukrainian user base and Ukrainian-language support. Focused on messenger-based sales — integrates with Viber, Telegram, WhatsApp, Instagram DMs.

**Why Ukrainian sales teams choose amoCRM:**
- Ukrainian language interface ✅
- Messenger integration — manage all Ukrainian messenger conversations in CRM
- Viber and Telegram bots built-in
- Visual sales pipeline
- Mobile app works well offline

**Ukrainian context:** Ukrainian buyers increasingly prefer messenger communication (Viber, Telegram) over email. amoCRM's messenger integrations are purpose-built for this reality.

**Pricing:** Base $15/user/month

---

## 4. Pipedrive — Best Sales-Focused CRM

Pipedrive is the most sales-focused CRM — built for teams that sell, not for marketing or customer service. Visual pipeline, activity reminders, and sales reporting make it popular with Ukrainian B2B sales teams.

**Why sales teams choose Pipedrive:**
- Visual Kanban pipeline — see every deal at a glance
- Activity-based selling — focus on next action, not just deal stage
- Email integration — log all correspondence automatically
- Sales forecasting and reporting
- Excellent mobile app

**Pricing:** Essential $14/user/month, Advanced $29/user/month

---

## 5. Zoho CRM — Best Budget Paid CRM

Zoho CRM offers enterprise-level features at SMB pricing. The free plan covers 3 users — useful for very small Ukrainian teams. Paid plans are more affordable than HubSpot or Salesforce.

**Features:**
- Lead scoring
- Workflow automation
- AI sales assistant (Zia)
- Social media integration
- Territory management
- 500+ integrations

**Free plan:** 3 users, basic CRM features
**Pricing:** Standard $14/user/month, Professional $23/user/month

---

## 6. Notion as CRM — Zero Cost Option

For Ukrainian startups with minimal budget, Notion databases can function as a lightweight CRM:

- Contacts database with custom properties
- Deal pipeline as a Kanban board
- Task linking to contacts and deals
- Document storage alongside contact data

**Limitation:** Not a real CRM — no email tracking, no calling, no sales automation. Outgrows this setup quickly as team and lead volume grows.

**When to use:** Pre-revenue or very early stage where tracking 20-50 relationships in a structured way is all you need.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which CRM is most popular among Ukrainian businesses?**
A: Bitrix24 has the largest Ukrainian user base due to Ukrainian language support, unlimited free users, and comprehensive feature set. amoCRM is second most popular, particularly among sales teams communicating via messengers.

**Q: Do CRM systems support Ukrainian Cyrillic input?**
A: Yes — all major CRMs handle Unicode/Cyrillic correctly for contact names, notes, and custom fields. amoCRM and Bitrix24 have Ukrainian interfaces throughout.

**Q: Can Ukrainian businesses integrate CRM with Viber or Telegram?**
A: amoCRM has the best Viber and Telegram integration — messages from these platforms appear directly in the CRM deal/contact card. Bitrix24 also supports messenger integrations. HubSpot and Salesforce have limited CIS messenger support.

**Q: What's the best free CRM for a Ukrainian startup?**
A: HubSpot CRM free plan is the most capable free CRM globally — unlimited contacts, pipeline management, email tracking, and meeting scheduling. Bitrix24 free plan is better if you need Ukrainian language interface and unlimited users.

---

## Bottom Line

For most Ukrainian businesses: **HubSpot Free** (English, most capable free plan) or **Bitrix24** (Ukrainian interface, unlimited free users).

For Ukrainian sales teams focused on messenger communication: **amoCRM** (Viber/Telegram integration).

For B2B sales teams that want the cleanest pipeline experience: **Pipedrive**.
