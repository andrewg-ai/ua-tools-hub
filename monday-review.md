## Honest Downsides

**Expensive at scale:** At $12-24/seat, Monday.com gets costly for larger Ukrainian teams. ClickUp at $7/user is significantly cheaper with more features.

**Weak documentation:** Monday.com is not designed for documentation or knowledge management. Notion is far better for wikis and company handbooks.

**Limited free plan:** Only 2 seats free — makes it hard to evaluate with a real team before paying.
