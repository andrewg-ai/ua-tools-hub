## Keyword Research

### Semrush Keywords
- **26 billion keywords** in database — largest available
- Ukrainian language keywords with accurate search volumes
- Keyword Magic Tool generates thousands of related keywords
- Intent classification (informational, navigational, commercial, transactional)
- CPC data for paid search planning
- Keyword difficulty score with detailed ranking analysis

### Ahrefs Keywords
- **20+ billion keywords** in database
- Ukrainian keyword data with volume estimates
- Keywords Explorer shows traffic potential (not just volume)
- Click-through rate data — shows how many searchers actually click
- "Also rank for" and "Also talk about" keyword suggestions
- Parent topic grouping reduces keyword cannibalization

**Key difference:** Semrush shows more keywords. Ahrefs shows better context for each keyword (CTR data, traffic potential).

**Winner: Semrush (volume) | Ahrefs (quality) 🔑**

## Backlink Analysis

### Semrush Backlinks
- 43 trillion backlinks indexed
- Authority Score (AS) — Semrush's domain authority metric
- Backlink gap analysis — compare your link profile vs competitors
- Link building tool with prospect management
- Toxic link identification and disavow file creation

### Ahrefs Backlinks
- **35+ trillion backlinks** but updated every 15-30 minutes (most fresh)
- Domain Rating (DR) — industry-standard authority metric
- Best visualization of link profile growth over time
- Content Explorer finds most-linked content in any niche
- Link intersect tool (similar to Semrush's gap analysis)

**Key difference:** Ahrefs' Domain Rating (DR) is the most widely recognized authority metric in the industry — when Ukrainian agencies report to clients, DR is the standard. Ahrefs backlink data is also fresher.

**Winner: Ahrefs 🔗**

## Competitor Analysis

### Semrush Competitor Research
- Domain Overview — complete traffic, keywords, backlinks snapshot
- Traffic Analytics — estimates competitor website visits
- Organic Research — every keyword a competitor ranks for
- Advertising Research — competitor PPC keywords and ad copies
- Market Explorer — discover new competitors in your niche

### Ahrefs Competitor Research
- Site Explorer — organic traffic, keywords, backlinks
- Content Gap — keywords competitors rank for but you don't
- Link Intersect — sites linking to competitors but not you
- No PPC competitor research

**Key difference:** Semrush includes paid search (PPC) competitor analysis — critical for Ukrainian e-commerce businesses running Google Ads. Ahrefs focuses on organic only.

**Winner: Semrush (broader scope) 🏆**
