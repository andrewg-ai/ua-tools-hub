## The Pipedrive Philosophy: Activity-Based Selling

Most CRMs focus on deal stages. Pipedrive focuses on activities — the actions salespeople take to move deals forward.

**The insight:** You can't control whether a prospect buys. You CAN control whether you make the call, send the follow-up, schedule the demo. Pipedrive keeps salespeople focused on activities, which drives results.

**For Ukrainian B2B sales teams:** This philosophy resonates with consultative selling — relationship-based, activity-driven, focused on consistent execution rather than hoping deals close themselves.

## Key Features

### Visual Pipeline
Drag deals between stages in a visual Kanban board. Color-coding shows deal health — green for on-track, red for overdue. At a glance, Ukrainian sales managers see exactly where every deal stands.

### Smart Contact Data
Pipedrive automatically enriches contact profiles with data from LinkedIn and public sources. Less manual data entry for Ukrainian sales teams.

### Email Integration
Two-way sync with Gmail and Outlook — all email correspondence automatically logged to the relevant deal. No more switching between email and CRM.

### Sales Assistant (AI)
AI-powered recommendations: "You haven't contacted this lead in 7 days — send a follow-up?" Keeps deals from falling through cracks.

### LeadBooster Add-on
Chatbot for website lead capture + prospector for finding new leads. Ukrainian B2B companies add LeadBooster to capture inbound interest automatically.
