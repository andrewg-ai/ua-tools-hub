## The 5 Mangools Tools Explained

### 1. KWFinder — Keyword Research
The core tool. Enter any keyword and get search volume, keyword difficulty (0-100), trend data, and SERP analysis. Particularly useful for finding low-competition Ukrainian keywords in English-language niches.

**Best for:** Finding keywords your competitors haven't targeted yet.

### 2. SERPChecker — SERP Analysis
Analyze the top 10 results for any keyword. See DA, PA, backlink counts, and social signals for each competitor. Helps you understand if you can realistically rank for a keyword.

### 3. SERPWatcher — Rank Tracking
Track your website's keyword rankings daily. Set up tracking for your domain and get email alerts when positions change. The Dominance Index shows your overall visibility growth over time.

### 4. LinkMiner — Backlink Analysis
Analyze backlinks for any URL. See link strength, anchor text, and whether links are active. Good for finding link building opportunities in your niche.

### 5. SiteProfiler — Site Analysis
Get a complete SEO profile for any domain — authority score, top content, backlink profile, and competitor comparison.
