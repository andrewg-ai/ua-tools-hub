## 1. GetResponse — Best for Ukrainian Businesses

GetResponse has been a favorite among Ukrainian marketers for years. Polish company, strong Eastern European market focus, Ukrainian language interface available.

**Why Ukrainian businesses choose it:**
- Ukrainian language interface — significant advantage for non-English teams
- Strong Cyrillic character handling
- Webinar functionality built-in (no separate tool)
- Landing page builder included
- Marketing automation on mid-tier plans
- Accepts Ukrainian payment methods

**Best features:**
- AI email generator writes campaigns in Ukrainian
- Conversion funnel builder for end-to-end campaigns
- SMS marketing available
- E-commerce integrations (WooCommerce, Shopify)

**Free plan:** 500 contacts, 2,500 emails/month, 1 landing page

**Paid plans:** Email Marketing $19/month, Marketing Automation $59/month

---

## 2. eSputnik — Ukrainian-Built Platform

eSputnik is a Ukrainian-built marketing automation platform, founded in Kyiv. Designed specifically for the Ukrainian and CIS markets.

**Why it matters:**
- Built by Ukrainians for Ukrainian businesses
- Ukrainian and Russian language support throughout
- Strong understanding of local market needs
- Local support team in Ukraine
- Omnichannel: email + SMS + Viber + Web Push in one platform

**Viber messaging:** Critical differentiator for Ukrainian market. Viber is more popular than WhatsApp among Ukrainian mobile users. eSputnik integrates Viber business messaging — no other major email platform does this as well.

**Pricing:** Free plan available. Paid from $15/month by contact tier.

---

## 3. Mailchimp — Most Popular Worldwide

Mailchimp is the world's most used email marketing platform with 12+ million users. Generous free plan and extensive integrations make it the default choice for new businesses.

**Strengths:**
- Best-in-class template library
- Free plan: 500 contacts, 1,000 emails/month
- Integrates with everything (Shopify, WordPress, Zapier, 300+ more)
- AI-powered subject line recommendations
- Audience segmentation on free plan

**Weaknesses for Ukrainian users:**
- Interface English-only
- Limited automation on free/basic plans
- Price increases significantly as list grows

**Pricing:** Free (500 contacts), Essentials $13/month, Standard $20/month

---

## 4. Brevo (Sendinblue) — Best for Transactional Email

Brevo excels at transactional emails — order confirmations, password resets, shipping notifications — alongside marketing campaigns. Pricing by email volume rather than contact count makes it economical for large lists with low send frequency.

**Why Ukrainian e-commerce uses it:**
- Unlimited contacts on all plans (pay by sends, not contacts)
- Transactional API for developers
- SMS integration (Ukrainian numbers)
- Excellent deliverability reputation
- GDPR compliance tools

**Pricing:** Free (300 emails/day), Starter $25/month (20,000 emails)

---

## 5. Klaviyo — Best for Ukrainian E-Commerce

Klaviyo is built specifically for e-commerce. Deep integrations with Shopify, WooCommerce, and PrestaShop make it the choice for Ukrainian online stores.

**Why e-commerce chooses Klaviyo:**
- Behavioral triggers — email based on what customer did on site
- Revenue attribution — see exact revenue generated by each campaign
- Predictive analytics — when customer will buy next, predicted lifetime value
- Pre-built e-commerce flows: welcome series, abandoned cart, post-purchase
- SMS + email in one platform

**For Ukrainian online stores:** Klaviyo's abandoned cart emails and post-purchase sequences are proven to increase revenue 15-30%. Ukrainian stores using Shopify integrate Klaviyo in minutes.

**Pricing:** Free (250 contacts, 500 emails/month), Email $45/month (1,000 contacts)

---

## 6. ActiveCampaign — Best Marketing Automation

ActiveCampaign has the most powerful automation builder in the industry — visual drag-and-drop workflows with hundreds of trigger/action combinations.

**For Ukrainian businesses with complex funnels:**
- CRM built into email platform
- Lead scoring — automatically score leads based on behavior
- Site tracking — trigger emails based on pages visited
- SMS + email + chat in one platform
- 870+ integrations

**Pricing:** No free plan. Starter $29/month, Plus $49/month

## 7. ConvertKit — Best for Ukrainian Content Creators

ConvertKit (now Kit) is designed for creators — bloggers, YouTubers, course creators, newsletter authors. Used widely by Ukrainian content creators targeting international audiences.

**Creator-specific features:**
- Free newsletter page (creator.com subdomain)
- Paid newsletters with Stripe integration
- Visual automation builder
- Landing pages and forms
- Commerce features for digital product sales

**Free plan:** 1,000 subscribers, unlimited emails, landing pages

**Pricing:** Newsletter free (1,000 contacts), Creator $29/month

---

## 8. MailerLite — Best Budget Option

MailerLite delivers 80% of Mailchimp's functionality at significantly lower cost. Popular among Ukrainian NGOs and small businesses with tight budgets.

**Why Ukrainian organizations choose it:**
- Most generous free plan (1,000 contacts, 12,000 emails/month)
- Simple, clean interface
- Good deliverability
- Landing pages and forms on free plan
- Automation on paid plans

**Pricing:** Free (1,000 contacts), Growing Business $9/month

## Email Marketing Checklist for Ukrainian Businesses

**Technical setup (one-time):**
- [ ] SPF record in DNS
- [ ] DKIM authentication
- [ ] DMARC policy
- [ ] Custom sending domain (not platform default)

**Legal requirements:**
- [ ] Unsubscribe link in every email
- [ ] Physical address in email footer
- [ ] Consent documented for each subscriber
- [ ] Privacy policy linked

**Campaign best practices:**
- [ ] Mobile-optimized template (70%+ opens on mobile in Ukraine)
- [ ] Test subject lines with A/B testing
- [ ] Segment lists by behavior and demographics
- [ ] Send at optimal times (Tuesday-Thursday, 10am-12pm local)

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Which email marketing platform works best for Ukrainian businesses?**
A: GetResponse and eSputnik are the most Ukraine-friendly platforms — both have Ukrainian language interfaces and understand local market needs. For e-commerce, Klaviyo delivers the best ROI. For tight budgets, MailerLite's free plan is the most generous.

**Q: Do email marketing platforms support Cyrillic characters?**
A: Yes — all major platforms (Mailchimp, GetResponse, Brevo, Klaviyo) correctly handle Ukrainian and Russian Cyrillic characters in subject lines and email body. Always test with a Cyrillic-heavy subject line before sending campaigns.

**Q: Can Ukrainian businesses pay for email marketing platforms?**
A: Yes. All platforms listed accept Visa/Mastercard from Ukrainian banks. GetResponse and eSputnik have specific experience with Ukrainian payment methods.

**Q: What's the best free email marketing tool for Ukraine?**
A: MailerLite offers the most generous free plan — 1,000 contacts and 12,000 emails/month free. eSputnik and GetResponse both have free tiers worth testing for Ukrainian-market focused campaigns.

For most Ukrainian businesses: **GetResponse** (Ukrainian interface, strong features) or **MailerLite** (best free plan, simple).
